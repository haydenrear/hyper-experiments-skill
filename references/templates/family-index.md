# Family: {{family}}

Created: {{created_at}}

This document is the **per-family planning and orchestration index** for
the `{{family}}` family. It is forward-looking: theories we're probing,
recommended configs, experiments we plan to run, open questions.

Completed facts (status, commands, final findings) belong in each
individual experiment's `results.md` and in the project-level
`experiments/experiments.md` ledger. This file captures the *theory of
the family* — what the family, collectively, currently believes.

Update this document every time an experiment in the family finishes,
every time a new proposed experiment is born, and every time a theory
is strengthened or weakened by fresh results.
